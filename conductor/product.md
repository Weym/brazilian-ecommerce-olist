# Product Guide: Olist Early Warning System

## Initial Concept
Transform historical logistics data into an early warning system that allows intervention before delivery and poor reviews. The model classifies orders at risk of receiving 1-2 stars **before** delivery happens, using only variables available at the time of order approval.

## Target Audience
- **Logistics Managers:** Monitoring overall logistics performance and identifying systemic issues.
- **Data Team:** Refining and optimizing predictive models to ensure accuracy and operational relevance.

## Core Features
- **Geographic Analysis:** In-depth mapping and analysis of logistics bottlenecks, focusing on specific Brazilian states (UFs) and critical shipping routes (e.g., SP -> Nordeste) to identify high-risk delivery patterns.
- **Early Risk Detection:** Utilizing machine learning to flag orders at risk of negative feedback due to logistics delays and other factors.

## Success Metrics
- **PR-AUC Optimization:** Balancing precision and recall to ensure the system provides high-quality alerts for operational intervention without overwhelming users with false positives.

## Strategic Goal
- **Reduce Bad Reviews:** Proactively addressing logistics failures to decrease the overall rate of 1-2 star reviews, thereby improving the marketplace's reputation and customer trust.
