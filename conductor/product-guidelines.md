# Product Guidelines: Olist Early Warning System

## Prose Style
- **Clear and Technical:** Use precise terminology for data science and logistics concepts while ensuring clarity for business users.
- **Action-Oriented:** Focus on providing insights that lead to direct interventions in the delivery process.
- **Objective:** Maintain a neutral and data-driven tone throughout the documentation and application.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Insight at a Glance:** The dashboard and alerts should prioritize key risks and bottlenecks to minimize time-to-insight.
- **Actionable Visualizations:** Every chart or map should contribute directly to understanding and resolving delivery risks.
- **Consistent Feedback:** Provide users with clear indicators of data quality and model confidence levels.

## Branding and Visual Style
- **Trustworthy and Professional:** Use a minimalist design that emphasizes data integrity and reliability.
- **Minimalist Palette:** A clean, data-focused color scheme (e.g., standard Streamlit aesthetics) to ensure information clarity.
- **Visual Hierarchies:** Clearly distinguish between critical alerts, secondary risks, and general logistics overview.
